# License Management

The License Management module helps you track software licenses, manage subscriptions, and ensure compliance across your organization. Stay on top of renewal dates and optimize your software spending.

## Key Features

- **License Tracking**: Maintain a complete inventory of all software licenses
- **Expiration Alerts**: Receive notifications before licenses expire
- **Assignment Management**: Assign licenses to users or assets
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Ensure you're not over or under-licensed
- **Cost Tracking**: Monitor software spending and subscription costs

## Quick Start

1. Navigate to the **Licenses** section from the main menu
2. Click **Create License** to add your first license
3. Fill in the license details (name, vendor, key, dates)
4. Save the license to add it to your inventory

## License Types

Fluxera supports various license types:

- **Perpetual**: One-time purchase with permanent usage rights
- **Subscription**: Recurring payment for continued access
- **Volume**: Multiple licenses purchased together
- **Trial**: Temporary evaluation licenses
- **Open Source**: Free and open-source software licenses

## License Statuses

Licenses are automatically categorized by their expiration status:

- **Active**: Valid and within the license period
- **Expiring Soon**: Less than 30 days until expiration
- **Expired**: Past the expiration date
- **Pending**: Awaiting activation or approval
