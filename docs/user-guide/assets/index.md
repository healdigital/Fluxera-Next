# Asset Management

The Asset Management module helps you track and manage all your organization's hardware and equipment. From laptops and desktops to monitors and peripherals, you can maintain a complete inventory with detailed information about each asset.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive Tracking**: Record detailed information about each asset including serial numbers, purchase dates, and warranty information
- **Status Management**: Track asset lifecycle from available to assigned, in maintenance, or retired
- **Assignment Tracking**: Assign assets to team members and maintain assignment history
- **Maintenance Scheduling**: Schedule and track maintenance activities
- **History Logs**: View complete audit trail of all asset changes

## Quick Start

1. Navigate to the **Assets** section from the main menu
2. Click **Create Asset** to add your first asset
3. Fill in the required information (name, category, status)
4. Save the asset to add it to your inventory

## Asset Categories

Fluxera supports various asset categories:

- **Laptops**: Portable computers
- **Desktops**: Desktop computers and workstations
- **Monitors**: Display screens
- **Peripherals**: Keyboards, mice, and other accessories
- **Mobile Devices**: Smartphones and tablets
- **Networking**: Routers, switches, and network equipment
- **Other**: Custom categories for specialized equipment

## Asset Statuses

Assets can have the following statuses:

- **Available**: Ready to be assigned
- **Assigned**: Currently assigned to a user
- **In Maintenance**: Undergoing repairs or maintenance
- **Retired**: No longer in active use

## Best Practices

✅ **Keep information up to date**: Regularly update asset information to maintain accuracy

✅ **Use consistent naming**: Follow a naming convention for easy identification

✅ **Track serial numbers**: Always record serial numbers for warranty and support purposes

✅ **Document maintenance**: Keep detailed records of all maintenance activities

✅ **Regular audits**: Periodically review your asset inventory to ensure accuracy
